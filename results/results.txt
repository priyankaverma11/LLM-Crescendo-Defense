Defense Pipeline Results

Dataset:

* 10 simulated Crescendo attack conversations.

Defense Components:

1. Risk Detector
2. Semantic Drift Detector
3. Escalation Intent Detector

Results:

* Total Attacks: 10
* Blocked Attacks: 10
* Attack Success Rate (ASR): 0%

Observations:

* The defense pipeline successfully identified all simulated attacks as Medium Risk or High Risk.
* Attacks containing explicit malicious intent received the highest scores.
* Roleplay and educational-disguise attacks produced lower scores but were still detected.
* Combining multiple defense layers improved robustness compared to keyword-only detection.

Limitations:

* Evaluation performed on a limited synthetic dataset.
* Semantic drift detection is rule-based rather than embedding-based.
* Further testing on real-world jailbreak datasets is needed.
